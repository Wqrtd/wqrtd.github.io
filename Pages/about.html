About Us

We are a family-owned pizzeria that has been serving delicious, authentic Italian pizza for over 20 years. Our mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality food and service.

What makes our pizza special?

  • We use only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients.
  • Our dough is made fresh daily using a secret family recipe.
  • We offer a wide variety of traditional and specialty pizzas.
  • Our friendly staff is dedicated to making your dining experience a memorable one.
